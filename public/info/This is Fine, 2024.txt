This is Fine, 2024
Glazed ceramic
46 x 37 cm (approx. 18 × 14.5 in)
5 kg (approx. 11 lb)


Adapted from "This Is Fine" (2013) by KC Green
This ceramic sculpture by Dimitria Barrows transforms KC Green's iconic internet meme into a contemporary memento mori, translating the meme's sardonic optimism into a lasting object of reflection. The handmade glazed vase reimagines the calm dog amid flames on one side and his skeletal form on the other - a Baroque‑like meditation on humor, denial, and decay.
A crown of lush, hand‑sculpted roses and sharp thorns rises from the burnt rim, merging beauty and pain while evoking the vanitas tradition. Drawing from internet culture and historical ceramic craftsmanship, Barrows blends Baroque drama with the tactile ritual of clay. What once adorned domestic interiors now becomes commentary on fragility in the age of digital permanence.
Barrows' work turns digital ephemera into an enduring meditation on resilience - quietly asking what remains when the fire becomes our new normal.
