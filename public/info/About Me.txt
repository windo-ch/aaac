
Contemporary ceramic artist, Dimitria Barrows (ART AS A CONSEQUENCE - AAAC) spent a decade working full-time in the art industry—at Gagosian, Galerie Thaddaeus Ropac, and among others—handling exhibitions, sales, and artists’ studios—before returning to her own practice in 2024. Now Barrows makes ceramics, including functional sculptural vessels.

Based in Zug, Switzerland—better known as Crypto Valley—Barrows creates handmade stoneware ceramics—vases and vessels exploring value formation in internet memes, crypto culture, economics, and onchain dynamics.

Barrows bridges the contemporary art scene with the web3 space. Each artwork is unique, boldly glazed, and created with utility in mind — it mirrors how we assign (and verify) worth, a question she watched play out in blue-chip galleries and now watches play out onchain. Barrows’ artworks are NFC-chipped and onchain-registered; these conceptual ceramics exist as physical-digital twins.

Barrows graduated from ZHdK in Zurich (BA Fine Arts, 2015).
